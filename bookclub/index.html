Reading

At the beginning of 2021, I set myself the goal of reading one book a week (inspired by this video).

And since then, with a few exceptions, I’ve stuck to it. This year I relaxed the rules a bit and allowed myself more time for books and sometimes put books aside again if they weren’t the right ones, but I (always) loved to read and still read quite a lot.

That’s why I’m sharing my reading lists of the last few years here, and maybe the odd book review in the future if I feel like it.

I read books sometimes in German, sometimes in English, sometimes on paper, often on my reader (a Tolino), and sometimes I relax and listen to an audio book. Whatever I feel like.
