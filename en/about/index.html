82MHz

Hi, my name is Andi, I go by 82MHz online.

Why 82MHz? Well one part comes from my year of birth, the other from the fact that I studied electrical engineering with a specialization in audio and telecommunications… get it?

Anyway, I’m an embedded software developer in my day job and outside of that I am most interested in music (with lots of guitars or lots of synths), coding, free software, retro computing and generally tinkering around with all kinds of tech. I often start projects and have a hard time finishing them, so I hope posting about them online will help my procrastination habits a little.

I also read a lot, mostly SciFi or non-fiction about psychology, tech, society… whatever I find fascinating at the moment.
